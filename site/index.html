PIX

Your fairy companion on the Rift

A free desktop app that reads your League client and turns each session into a head start — your rank and stats, champion‑select builds and runes, a live objective coach, and honest post‑game feedback. All on your machine, with your own Riot API key.

What it does

Everything you check before and during a game — in one place

PIX watches the client and shows the right thing at the right moment: the lobby, champion select, the game itself.

🏆

Profile & rank

Level, ranked tier with emblem, LP, win rate and your peak league — at a glance.

📜

Match history & stats

Recent games with KDA and per‑champion win rates, plus ARAM and ranked filters.

🎯

Champion‑select assistant

Recommended picks for your role, runes, summoner spells, item build and a full skill‑order grid.

🐉

Live coach

Dragon, Herald and Baron timers on the Rift, and composition analysis in ARAM.

✨

Mastery & build explorer

Your champion mastery, and a searchable explorer for any champion's build and runes by lane.

🧘

Post‑game & tilt check

Plain‑language summary of your last game, one thing to improve, and a gentle nudge on losing streaks.

⚡

Apply to client

Push the suggested runes and item set straight into the League client with one click.

🌐

Español & English

Switch languages any time from the top bar — the whole interface follows.

🔒

Local & private

No shared server. Your key and data stay on your device and are never sent to anyone.

Get started

Up and running in three steps

No account to create, nothing to configure on a server. It runs next to your League client.

01

Download & install

Grab the installer for your system and open PIX. The window opens next to your game.

02

Paste your Riot API key

Get a free key at developer.riotgames.com and paste it in Settings. It's stored locally.

03

Open League and play

PIX detects your account and game state automatically — profile, builds and coaching appear on their own.

🔒

Your data stays yours

PIX runs entirely on your computer. It uses your own Riot API key, stored locally, to read your profile and matches — and nothing else. There is no PIX server, no account, and no tracking.
